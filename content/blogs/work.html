Academic Work

Research During my Undergraduate, as my final project, my study group researched for The Antecedents and Lead Indicators to a Global Financial Crisis.

It was about critically analyze the various investment avenues and their interdependencies with the stock market, in order to establish certain movements in them as indicators for the stock market crash.

Multiple variables had been analyzed, with the dependent variable being the S&P500 index, as an assumption that the US has been the epicenter for most crises has been made. A descriptive research with secondary data was carried out, correlational tools were used on data for the last 40 years, covering 3 major crises: the 1980s recession, the dot-com bubble, and the sub-prime mortgage crisis. The research was successful at establishing relationships between the variables.
